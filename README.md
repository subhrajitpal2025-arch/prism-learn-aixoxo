# 🚀 OrbitEdu – Your AI-Powered Learning Universe

OrbitEdu is a next-generation AI-powered EdTech platform designed to revolutionize the way students learn, focus, and grow. Built with modern web technologies and intelligent AI integrations, OrbitEdu provides a complete learning ecosystem that helps students stay productive, clear doubts instantly, and achieve their academic goals.

---

## 🌟 Overview

Students today often struggle with:

* Lack of personalized study guidance
* Difficulty maintaining focus
* Delayed doubt resolution
* Inefficient revision methods
* Limited progress tracking

OrbitEdu solves these challenges through a unified AI-driven platform that combines learning, productivity, and performance analytics into a single experience.

### 📊 Smart Dashboard

* Centralized learning dashboard

AI-generated quizzes with:

* Multiple difficulty levels

### 🃏 Smart Flash Cards

* Rapid revision resources
